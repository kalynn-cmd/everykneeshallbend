About Us

Every Knee Shall Bend exists to assist Catholic Churches that desire to restore or install communion altar rails. We provide direct grant support to parishes seeking to deepen reverence within the Sacred Liturgy.

Fostering Reverence

The restoration of altar rails addresses a growing desire for greater solemnity in worship. Kneeling remains a profound and humble act of adoration before Our Eucharistic Lord.

Our grant is provided directly to the church to help support these sacred restoration efforts.

Request Assistance

If you are a Pastor or Bishop who is interested in financial assistance for an altar rail project, we encourage you to apply for a grant. Grants are made on an ongoing basis as funds become available. Please fill out the form below to begin the process and learn more.

Thank You

Thank you for filling out this information. We will be in touch to ask for additional information and to give you an approximate funding date, such as whether funds are available at this time or whether we are on a waiting list cycle.

Please note we will need you to gather the following additional information:

  • A letter from the Pastor explaining what he hopes to accomplish in his parish by installing altar rails
  • A letter of support from the local Bishop
  • Any estimates you have received from contractors
  • Pictures of the current church altar area
  • Any mock ups of how it will look after the altar rails are installed
  • Any pictures of altar rails you hope to install

Thank you and we will be in touch shortly!

In general so you know: If you are approved for the grant, money is allocated as invoices come due, so that the church is not given a lump sum at any one time.

This is not aimed at churches currently under construction. We hope money for altar rails will be raised during their capital campaign.

We are not able to speak to the media at this time. We are a group of benefactors who want the attention to be on humble devotion to our Eucharistic Lord, not about who we are.
