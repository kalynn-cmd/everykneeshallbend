About Us

Every Knee Shall Bend exists to assist Catholic Churches that desire to restore or install communion altar rails. We provide direct grant support to parishes seeking to deepen reverence within the Sacred Liturgy.

Fostering Reverence

The restoration of altar rails addresses a growing desire for greater solemnity in worship. Kneeling remains a profound and humble act of adoration before Our Lord.

Our grant is provided directly to the church to help support these sacred restoration efforts.
